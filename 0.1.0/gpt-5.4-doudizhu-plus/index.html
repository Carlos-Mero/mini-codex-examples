Quick Rules

Common supported Dou Dizhu combinations.

  • Singles, pairs, triples, triple + single, triple + pair
  • Straights of 5+ cards (3 up to A, no 2 or jokers)
  • Consecutive pairs of 3+ pairs
  • Airplanes: consecutive triples, with or without wings
  • Four with two singles / four with two pairs
  • Bombs and the rocket (both jokers)
  • Bombs beat every non-bomb; rocket beats everything
